Pasta salad

Description

Pasta salad makes a great-ahead dish! With rich flavour

pasta salad is very good option to go with in barbecue session.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound tri-colored spiral pasta
  • 1 (16 ounce) bottle Italian-style salad dressing
  • 6 tablespoons salad seasoning mix
  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes, diced
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1/2 yellow bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 (2.25 ounce) can black olives, chopped

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.

  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook pasta in the boiling water,

    stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 10 to 12 minutes;

    rinse under cold water and drain.

  3. Whisk Italian dressing and salad spice mix together until smooth. Combine pasta, tomatoes,

    bell peppers, and olives in a salad bowl.

  4. Pour dressing over salad and toss to coat.
  5. Refrigerate salad, 8 hours to overnight.
  6. Enjoy!
